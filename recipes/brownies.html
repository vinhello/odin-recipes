Brownies Recipe

Description

Brownies are a type of chocolate cake that is made with cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, and flour. They are typically baked in a pan and then cut into squares.

Ingredients

  • Cocoa Powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Mix the cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, and flour
  2. Bake the mixture in a pan
  3. Cut the mixture into squares
